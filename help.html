HELP for playing DecDec BINGO:

 

Never played? Just not sure how? We're here to help. It's as easy as 1, 2, 3, 4.

1: Grab a card -- either physical or virtual -- and some markers (if playing on a physical card).

2: Listen for the numbers to be called.

3: If a called number is on your card, mark it.

4: If you get "BINGO", "Four Corners", or "Blackout" call it out (see below for what those mean).

 

NOTE:

The center square is special: You get to use that square for "free". It can be used for "BINGO" on either of the diagonals, the center row, or the center ("N") column.

 

WAYS TO WIN:

"BINGO": All five numbers in a single row, single column, or one of the diagonals.

"Four Corners": The four numbers in the corners of the board.

"Blackout": All the numbers on the board + "free".

You may only win one of each kind of prize. (NOTE: This rule is subject to change without notice)

You may need to bring a special prize for the "Blackout" prize pool to be eligible to win a "Blackout" prize.

 

WHAT'S UP WITH THE NUMBERS?

The pool of numbers to be called consists of those (base 10) numbers from 1 through 75 (inclusive) broken down into groups of 15.

Column "B": 1-15
Column "I": 16-30
Column "N": 31-45
Column "G": 46-60
Column "O": 61-75
